  • What time should I get to the club?

    Most club events start at 11pm and don't get busy until 1am, continuing until 6am. Arriving at Midnight is considered fairly early. You can generally arrive whenever you want if you have a ticket, unless the ticket specifies otherwise.

    The best way to deal with this is to eat light at around 5 or 6pm, then have a snooze, wake up at 10ish and eat a bit more, have a few drinks, then head out. Trying to do an ibiza club night after a full day can be a challenge.

  • Which town should I visit?

    Ibiza town is the main town, it's very multicultural, lots of Germans and Italians, some brits. The port area is excellent in the evening, both for meals and bars.

    San Antonio is heavily English, and bit on the grotty side in places, but the sunset strip outside Cafe Mambo is excellent at twilight, and the West End is full of bars and can be fun/chaotic.

    Santa Eularia is a much quieter more traditionally spanish seaside town, very good place to go out for a bite to eat, and the local beaches are great.

  • Where are the clubs?

    The majority are in and around Ibiza Town. Pacha and Chinois are near the port. DC10, Hi and Ushuaia are on the southern end of Ibiza Town near the airport. Cova Santa is further south yet, 10 minutes outside of town. Amnesia is directly between Ibiza Town and San Antonio. Akasha is just north of Santa Eularia and Eden is in central San Antonio.
